Benefits of Duck Meat:

  • Duck meat is a good source of protein. It contains niacin, phosphorus, riboflavin, iron, zinc, vitamin B6 and thiamine. In addition, there is a small amount of vitamin B12 and magnesium.
  • Duck meat, including skin, is also high in fat and cholesterol. Duck meat has more fat than beef. These fats contain saturated fats as well as unsaturated fats. Saturated fats contain cholesterol.
